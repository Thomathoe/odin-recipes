Tuna Salad

Description

Tuna salad is a classic go-to for quick lunches and snacks.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of tuna
  • 2 tbsp. sour cream
  • 1 tbsp. mayonnaise
  • 1/2 small red onion
  • 2 tbsp. lemon juice
  • Possibly dill or chives

Steps

  1. Pour the tuna into a bowl together with the sour cream and mayonnaise and mash it well
  2. Finely chop the red onion and add it to the tuna salad
  3. Season to taste with salt, pepper and lemon juice
